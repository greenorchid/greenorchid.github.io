# Digital Futures: Foundations First, Then Scale AI

At a recent Digital Futures session at Salesforce Tower, with perspectives from IBM, AWS, Accenture, and Salesforce, one message stood out:

AI success is not defined by the technology itself—but by the foundations we build around it.

Despite rapid investment and experimentation, up to 95% of AI pilots still fail to scale (MIT NANDA 2025). The gap is no longer innovation—it’s execution.

## Section 1: Skills & Mindset – The Real Transformation

AI is reshaping how work gets done, not just the tools we use.

The conversation reinforced that roles are evolving rather than disappearing, and that AI’s true value lies in augmenting human decision-making. The challenge is not access to technology—but adoption, mindset, and behaviour.

> AI won’t take your job—the person who knows how to use AI will.

> This is a workforce management shift as much as a technology shift.

To respond effectively, organisations need to:

- Invest in continuous upskilling, not one-off training
- Embed AI into everyday workflows
- Encourage ownership and experimentation across teams

This also requires breaking down silos and moving toward shared accountability. AI cannot succeed in isolated teams—it must be integrated into how the organisation operates as a whole.

## Section 2: Responsible AI – The Foundation for Scale

A consistent theme across all speakers was clear: responsible AI must come first.

Too often, governance, risk, and compliance are treated as an afterthought. In practice, they are the enablers of scale.

> Responsible AI isn’t just ethics—it’s trust, resilience, and compliance by design.

> You can’t retrofit responsibility at scale.

Successful AI implementation depends on:

- Clean, trusted, and well-governed data
- Clear ownership and accountability
- Built-in risk and compliance frameworks from day one
- Alignment to real customer value

Without these foundations, even the most promising AI use cases struggle to move beyond pilot into production.

## Conclusion

We are moving from AI experimentation to execution.

The takeaway is simple:

- Technology enables AI
- People adopt AI
- Foundations scale AI

Focusing on skills, mindset, and responsible foundations today is what will determine whether AI delivers meaningful impact tomorrow.
